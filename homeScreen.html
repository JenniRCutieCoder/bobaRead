About bobaRead

Learn about this new digital library

Unique Storylines with Choices

Each story follows a unique storyline where readers can make choices that influence the storyline leading to different endings each time they play. This allows for a more enjoyable play session and one where the reader will always be able to have fun throughout.

Different Learning Topics

Each story teaches the reader about different topic such as Math and History but also more career-specific topics such as Coding and Law. The reader will also have to answer questions throughout the story, helping them learn more about the topic taught in the story.

User-Friendly Web Design

bobaRead is an interactive, gender-neutral website that works to provide easy functionality and readability to readers of all types. The website layout is designed to be entertaining with lots of animation and enjoyable for readers of all ages to use.

Topic Progress Tracker Profile

Readers can keep track of how well they are doing with the topic progress tracker. This progress tracker logs in how many questions you got right based on the topics presented in the story. With this, the reader can see what topics they need most work on.

Customizable Playground

To help encourage more reading, readers can access the Shop where they can buy little items for their playground with coins they earned from playing the stories. This inspires more creative and lets readers use their imagination when designing their playgrounds.
